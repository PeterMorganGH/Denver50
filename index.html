50 places to eat and experience around Denver, Colorado

Rosenberg's Bagels

Rosenberg's Bagels bring New York-style bagels to Colorado. With a large variety of bagel flavors, cream cheeses, spreads, and fish you can create your own custom sandwhich or choose from one of the many options on the menu. Get here early or be prepared to wait a bit for your meal as these bagels draw a crowd.
